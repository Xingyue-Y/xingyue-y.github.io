Welcome to my homepage!

I’m currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at The University of Texas at Dallas. Prior to this, I earned my B.E. in Automation from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (Sept. 2020–Jun. 2024), and spent my senior year as an exchange student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UCSB (Sept. 2023–Jun. 2024).

Research interests: Intelligent power & energy systems (AI) — including BMS‑based storage optimization and SOH prediction, power electronics & grid‑integrated renewables; secondary interests in transportation control & optimization, and soft‑robotics real‑time control & feedback.

Current research projects: Machine‑learning–driven battery health diagnostics and multi‑sources physics‑informed modeling.

I am co‑author of one conference paper and hold one patent: “A Flexible Wearable Supernumerary Robotic Limb for Chronic Stroke Patients“.
